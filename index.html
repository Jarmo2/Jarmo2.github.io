Uncertainty is the very condition
to impel man to unfold his powers.

When the world was in flux as of March 2020, I was undauntedly hammering data into my Excel sheets in the audit. While the world stood still in many other professions, I was immersed in work. I realised it was time for a change. I decided to work on my digital skills while enjoying my life as much as possible. In September 2020, I finally started my journey. I discovered the Canary Islands, green Galicia and immersed myself in the world of programming.

Since October, I have now been working at the intersection of audit and the digital world. In my work, I got to know agile project methods and pushed ahead with my own developments. Now I can deal with Git, CICD pipelines and issues from cloud computing.

I continue learning with the help of openHPI, brilliant.org, datacamp.com and exercism.org.
